The goal is complete all the 5 WORDS

All words have locked letters, these letters need to compound the word.

Each letter gives you points, those points follow the rules bellow:

  • 2 points for each unique letter;
  • 1 points for each repeated letter (once)
  • 0 points for each repeated letter (twice or more)

Examples

The letter W is in the word and in the correct spot.

The letter I is in the word but in the wrong spot.

The letter U is not in the word in any spot.
