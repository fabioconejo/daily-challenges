The goal is complete all the 5 WORDS

The list have locked letters, these letters need to compound the words.

Each letter gives you points, these points follow the rules bellow:

  • 2 points for each unique letter;
  • 1 point for each repeated letter (once);
  • 0 points for each repeated letter (twice or more).

Repeated words are not allowed in same day.

Every day a new 5 WORDS list to complete!
